We are excited to announce the inaugural Toronto Knife Show, taking place on April 26, 2025, at the Four Points by Sheraton Airport Hotel in Mississauga. This event brings together knife enthusiasts, craftsmen, and collectors who share a passion for knives and related crafts. Whether you're a vendor, exhibitor, or an attendee, this gathering promises to be an unforgettable experience.

At this one-day event, you'll meet:

  • Custom knife makers specializing in fixed and folding blades
  • Artisans creating both artistic and functional knives
  • Forgers and smiths demonstrating traditional and modern techniques
  • Creators of fine, custom, and one-of-a-kind blades
  • Collectors of antique, folding, rare, and unique knives
  • Crafters of knife-related accessories like leather goods and fur items
  • Suppliers of materials and tools for knife making

The Toronto Knife Show is open to the public and offers a platform for makers to showcase their work, share their knowledge, and engage with a community that appreciates their dedication to the craft. Whether exhibiting for sale or simply displaying your creations, this is your chance to connect with fellow enthusiasts and buyers.
