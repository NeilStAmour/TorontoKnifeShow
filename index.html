Welcome to the Toronto Knife Show

We are excited to announce the second annual Toronto Knife Show, taking place on April 18, 2026, at the Four Points by Sheraton Toronto Airport in Mississauga.

Our 2025 debut was a resounding success, drawing a full house and a remarkable community of makers and collectors. We're thrilled to return to the same venue in 2026, building on that momentum with even more outstanding work on display.
